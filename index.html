Organic farming (OF) is a farming system that uses environmentally friendly methods of weed, pest, and disease control. The principles and practices of OF have been expressed in the standards of International Federation of Organic Agriculture Movements (IFOAM) as the principle of health, ecology, fairness, and care. The organic movement began after 1920, as a reaction by individual agricultural scientists and farmers against industrialized agriculture.
